What is Port Chuu?

Port Chuu (PC) is an online community founded at the dawn of 2020. Thank you for visiting us.

We are a friendly, social Discord community. We also have a custom semi-vanilla Minecraft server. Pull up a chair and stay a while! The goal of this server is to create a welcoming and friendly atmosphere where everyone can be comfortable.

Before you dive into the server, check up the rules. The rules are subject to change, so, in any case, use common sense, and avoid acting in bad faith.
